Operational Technology (OT) refers to the hardware and software systems used to manage, monitor, and control industrial operations, processes, and equipment. Unlike Information Technology (IT), which focuses on managing and processing data, OT is concerned with the physical devices and infrastructure that enable industrial activities. OT is commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, energy, transportation, and utilities to ensure the efficient, reliable, and safe operation of machinery and processes.
